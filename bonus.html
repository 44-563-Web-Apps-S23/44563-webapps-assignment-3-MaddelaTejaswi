Spiderman

Spider-Man is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko, he first appeared in the anthology comic book Amazing Fantasy

The following super powers it has :

  • Ability to cling to nearly any surface by his hands and feet
  • Internal “alarm” of sorts that warns him of impending danger
  • Peter Parker’s gifts from the spider bite include enhanced strength, stamina, and agility
